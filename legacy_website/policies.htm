Group Policies
   
Conferences
  • Think about what conferences you want to attend and discuss this with Ting.
  • If there is more than one, rank them in order of preference.
  • Before submitting your abstract, have Ting and the coauthors proofread it. Allow at least one week for proofreading.
  • No abstract should be submitted without consensus from your coauthors.

Travel Reimbursement
  • Make sure Ting has agreed to reimburse your expenses, and you know the procedure for reimbursement before you go on the trip.
  • Keep a copy of all forms and receipts.
  • If costs go above the following, talk to Ting about alternatives.
  • Airfare: $300 (round-trip). It is possible to reduce costs by booking the flight through a government agency.
  • Hotel room: $120 (per night)
  • Housing:
    • At APS, stay at the guest house. If more than two rooms would be required, it is more cost-effective to rent a suite.
    • At CHESS, stay at the guest house.
  • If you will be reimbursed through LBL, note that you will have to get authorization before you make your plans, then make arrangements through LBL's travel website.
  • When renting a car, do not buy insurance; it will not be reimbursed. Rather, use the UC insurance policy.
    • Check that you're using the UC insurance both when making a reservation and when getting the car.

Reference Letters
Please accompany a request for reference letters with:
  • A list of letters to write and deadlines for each
  • Complete addresses and contact information for each letter recipient
  • How to submit each letter: mail, email, online form, etc.
  • If applicable, a job or proposal description for each position you are applying to
  • Your current résumé or curriculum vitae
  • Any other information you feel is relevant or that should be included in the letters
  • An addressed, stamped envelope for each letter to be mailed.

Ordering
RES:
  • Place orders through http://resaccess.berkeley.edu/
    1. Click on "Purchasing and Reimbursement"
    2. In "Queue a purchase or reimbursement request for review and approval." select Ting's name
    3. Click on "Enter Supply Request"
    4. Select the vendor in the drop-down menu. If the vendor is not available, write the name in the comments section
    5. Fill out the first five columns for each item and hit "Calculate totals"
    6. Submit the request by clicking on "Queue Request for Review."
  • See Susan to be added to the approved purchasing personnel list.
LBL:
  1. Place orders by filling out the form here.
  2. See Ting for Project ID and signature
  3. Email the form to msdpurchasing@lbl.gov
