Types of Media and Serum

  • DMEM high glucose +10% FBS: standard medium for fibroblasts, PC3

  • Bovine Calf Serum (BCS): best serum supplement for fibroblast growth, although FBS works as well

  • Fetal Bovine Serum (FBS): standard serum supplement to media

  • Medium without phenol red: used for slide prep and viability

  • RPMI 1640 +10% FBS: medium for PC3
