Confocal Slide Prep


Protocol:

  1. Seed cells onto 8-well slide and culture til desired confluency
  2. Using phenol-red-free medium, dilute sample to desired concentration
  3. For incubation times >20 min, add sample first and add counter dyes in the last 20 min
    • I like to minimize the volume of sample to avoid dilution of serum; usually <10 μgL into 200 μL medium

    For incubation time =20 min, add sample and counter dyes together

    For incubation times <20 min, add counter dyes first (LysoTracker, Hoechst, etc) and add sample at desired time points
    • I like adding 2 μL of 100 μM LysoTracker and 2 μL of 500 μg/mL Hoechst into ~210 μL medium
  4. After entire slide is done, decant sample solution and wash 2x with pre-warmed PBS
  5. Add 150 μL 4% paraformaldehyde solution per well and allow incubation at room temp for 15-20 min
  6. Decant solution and wash 2x with sterile water
  7. Mount slide with Fluoromount-G and remove excess with paper towel; allow slide to dry for 5 min
  8. Examine with confocal microscope
