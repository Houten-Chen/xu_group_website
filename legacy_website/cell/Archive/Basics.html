Tissue Culture Basics

  • Keep everything sterile!
  • Spray surfaces and gloves with 70% EtOH
  • Always warm solutions in the 37°C water bath for at least 15 min prior to use
  • Be gentle when pipetting cells; avoid rapid aspiration and dispensal
  • Avoid bubbles at all cost!
  • Check cells daily for confluence/acidic changes to media; passage if necessary
  • Water level in the incubator & water bath needs to be at least half-full
  • Keep flasks and slides in the back of incubator to avoid temperature changes from opening the door
